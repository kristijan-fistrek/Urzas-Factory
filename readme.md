# Urza's Factory

Magic the Gathering deck building and analysis tool. Create your deck, learn new combos, track your data and analyse your decks performance.

### About The App

*You have a pet card that you want as a commander but don't know where to start with deck building?*

No worries, we got you covered. Urza's Factory (*name is a work in progress*) is there for you. All you need to do is **select your favorite commander** and **select the bracket of your deck** and we'll do the rest. 

*You have experience with deck building but would like to learn more about combos and how to improve your deck?*

No problem. Drop your decklist inside our deck analysis tool and we'll find all the potential combos within the deck and suggest new ones. You'll be able to single out a specific card and analyse how it synergises with the rest of your deck. 

*You are a cEDH enthusiast and need more detailed tools to analyse how your deck performs or how it would perform in its current state?*

Problem solved. Drop your decklist inside our deck analysis tool and we'll show you how your deck meta performs on major tournaments and calculate your winning percentage depending on your seat order.

___

What we also offer, not accounting for your experience and deck building skills, is the match tracking tool with which you can track the results of your games. 

- Enter your deck list.
- Enter your opponents deck lists.
- Enter your (and your opponents) opening hands.
- Enter your seat order.
- Enter the winning condition and who won the game.
- Write down your thoughts about the game and detail your game flow.

Analyse and reflect your matches to see how you can improve and what your thoughts were throughout the games with different decks.
